Interests

I am in pre-production on a documentary film about designer pet breeding practices and the Sphynx breed. I intend to investigate the relationship between for-profit breeding and ethical animal stewardship through the lens of the buyer seeking "ownership" of companion animals. What can be done to ensure the health and wellness of these special cats, and why is no one holding bad actors accountable?

I have pursued many hobbies and interests, from filmmaking to photography and performance arts. Lately, I have been learning how to use a sewing machine, dabbling in embroidery, and creating a giant cross-stitch mural of my beloved sphynx, Shmuley, who passed away this last year.
